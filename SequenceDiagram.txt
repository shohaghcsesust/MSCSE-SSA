@startuml
participant Patient
participant App
participant Backend
participant AuthService
participant RiskEngine
participant ScheduleEngine
participant DB
participant NotificationService

Patient -> App: Selects slot
App -> Backend: Request(slot)
Backend -> AuthService: Validate user
AuthService --> Backend: Validated
Backend -> RiskEngine: Get Risk Level
RiskEngine --> Backend: Risk Score
Backend -> ScheduleEngine: Assign Dentist
ScheduleEngine -> DB: Create Appointment
DB --> ScheduleEngine: Success
ScheduleEngine -> NotificationService: Notify parties
NotificationService --> ScheduleEngine: Sent
ScheduleEngine --> Backend: Assigned
Backend --> App: Booking Confirmation
App --> Patient: Confirmed
@enduml
